# White Ravens Invidious

White Ravens Invidious is an alternative way to watch YouTube videos — **without ads, without tracking, and without a Google account**. The service is based on the [Invidious](https://invidious.io/) project, which provides YouTube content through a lightweight, private interface.

!!! tip "Link"
    [White Ravens Invidious](https://invid.wrservices.link/)

**Key features:**

- :material-advertisements-off: **No ads** — watch videos {==without ad interruptions==}
- :material-shield-account: **Privacy** — no Google login required, no tracking
- :material-speedometer: **Lightweight interface** — fast loading, no unnecessary elements
- :material-playlist-play: **Subscriptions** — follow your favorite channels without a Google account

---

## How to use it?

### Browsing and watching

1. Go to [White Ravens Invidious](https://invid.wrservices.link/).
2. Type a search query in the search bar or browse suggested videos.
3. Click on a video to watch it — you can change the playback quality.

!!! info "No account needed"
    You can browse and watch videos without logging in. An account is only needed for saving subscriptions and settings.

### Registration (optional)

If you want to save subscriptions and settings:

1. Click **Register** in the menu.
2. Create an account — this is an account on our instance, {==a Google account is not required==}.
3. After logging in, you can add channels to subscriptions and create playlists.

## Transferring subscriptions from YouTube

If you have channels you follow on YouTube, you can easily transfer them to Invidious.

### Step 1: Download your subscriptions from YouTube

1. Go to [Google Takeout](https://takeout.google.com/takeout/custom/youtube) (you must be logged into your Google account).
2. Select YouTube data for export (including subscriptions).
3. Generate and download the archive.

!!! success "File format"
    YouTube exports subscriptions in **CSV** format, which Invidious accepts directly — you don't need to convert anything.

### Step 2: Import to Invidious

1. Log in to [White Ravens Invidious](https://invid.wrservices.link/).
2. Go to **Data Preferences** (in the menu).
3. Click **Import/Export data**.
4. Select the downloaded CSV file and click **Import**.
5. Your channels will appear in the subscriptions tab.

## Frequently asked questions

??? question "Is this legal?"
    Yes. Invidious uses YouTube's public API. You're watching the same videos, just through a different interface — without ads and tracking.

??? question "Do I need a Google account?"
    No. A Google account is not needed for browsing or watching videos. An account on our instance (optional) is only for saving subscriptions.

??? question "Why might some videos not work?"
    YouTube sometimes changes its internal mechanisms, which can temporarily affect Invidious functionality. In such cases, wait for an update or report the issue on Discord.

??? question "Can I watch in the highest quality?"
    Yes — you can choose the playback quality, including HD and Full HD, depending on availability on YouTube.
