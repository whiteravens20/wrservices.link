# Mastodon

Mastodon is a free social platform — an alternative to X (formerly Twitter). It works similarly: you publish short posts, follow others, comment, and share content. The key difference? Mastodon **is not controlled by a single company** — it consists of many independent servers that work together.

!!! tip "White Ravens profile on Mastodon"
    [mastodon.social/@whiteravens](https://mastodon.social/@whiteravens)

**Why Mastodon?**

- :material-account-group: **No single owner** — no company controls the platform or your data
- :material-bullhorn-outline: **No ads** — chronological timeline without algorithms promoting content
- :material-shield-account: **Privacy** — you decide who sees your posts
- :material-link-variant: **Fediverse** — you can communicate with users from other Mastodon servers

---

## How to get started?

### Step 1: Choose an instance and create an account

Mastodon is not a single website — it's a network of connected servers (called instances). It doesn't matter which one you choose — you'll be able to communicate with all users.

!!! info "What is an instance?"
    An instance is simply a Mastodon server run by an independent person or organization. It's like choosing an email provider — someone on Gmail can write to someone on Outlook. Same here: users of different instances can see each other.

Popular instances to choose from:

- [mastodon.social](https://mastodon.social/) — the largest, general instance
- [fosstodon.org](https://fosstodon.org/) — for open source fans
- [101010.pl](https://101010.pl/) — Polish instance

### Step 2: Set up your profile

Add a photo, a short description, and your interests — this will make it easier for others to find you.

### Step 3: Follow accounts

Start by following [@whiteravens](https://mastodon.social/@whiteravens), then discover other accounts through the search bar or local and global timelines.

### Step 4: Post and interact

- **Post** — write something (up to 500 characters) and publish
- :material-repeat: **Boost** — share a post with others (equivalent of a retweet)
- :material-star: **Favorite** — show that you like it
- :material-reply: **Reply** — join the discussion

## Frequently asked questions

??? question "Is Mastodon free?"
    Yes, completely. There are no fees or paid features.

??? question "Do I need to choose the same instance as White Ravens?"
    No. You can create an account on any instance — you'll still be able to follow and write to the White Ravens account. It's like email: you don't need to have an account with the same provider.

??? question "Can I move my account to another instance?"
    Yes. Mastodon allows account migration — your followers will automatically transfer with you.

??? question "How is Mastodon different from X (Twitter)?"
    Mastodon has no single owner, doesn't display ads, and doesn't manipulate the order of posts with algorithms. You see posts chronologically, from people you follow.
