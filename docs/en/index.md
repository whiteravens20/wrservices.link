## :material-shield-check: What makes us different?

- :material-eye-off: **Privacy** — we don't collect data, we don't track you, we don't profile you. Your data belongs to you.
- :material-lock: **Encryption** — wherever possible, data is encrypted in your browser before reaching the server.
- :material-open-source-initiative: **Open Source** — all our project code is public. Anyone can review it, report a bug, or suggest improvements.
- :material-currency-usd-off: **No costs** — all services are free. We maintain them through passion and community support.
- :material-account-group: **Community** — we are a group of people who believe in an open internet. Join us!

## :material-compass: Where to start?

You can find our services and social platforms in the **navigation menu** on the left side (or in the top menu on mobile). Here are a few suggestions to get started:

- :material-chat-processing: Want to chat? Check out our [**Element Chat**](chat.md) or drop by [**Discord**](discord.md).
- :material-lock: Want to securely share a file? Use [**Archivum Null**](archivum-null.md).
- :material-magnify: Looking for something online without tracking? Try our [**Meta Search**](search.md).
- :material-note-text: Need to quickly share text or code? [**PrivateBin**](privatebin.md) has you covered.

Each service has its own page with step-by-step instructions — written so that anyone can follow along.

## :material-open-source-initiative: We believe in Open Source

Free software is not just code — it's a philosophy of **transparency, collaboration, and trust**. Every one of our projects is open; anyone can review, improve, or suggest changes.

You don't need to be a developer to support this idea — it's enough to use open tools instead of closed corporate alternatives. :material-heart:{ .heart }
