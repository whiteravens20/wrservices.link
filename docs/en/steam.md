# Steam

Steam is the most popular platform for buying and playing games on PC. Besides the game store, it offers extensive social features — groups, forums, chats, and organizing multiplayer sessions. White Ravens has its own Steam group!

!!! tip "Join the group"
    [White Ravens Gaming on Steam](https://steamcommunity.com/groups/whiteravensgaming)

**What our group offers:**

- :material-controller-classic: **Multiplayer gaming** — find partners for your favorite games
- :material-forum: **Discussions** — talk about games, hardware, and news
- :material-calendar: **Events** — organized gaming sessions
- :material-account-group: **Community** — join a group of gamers with similar interests

---

## How to join?

### Step 1: Create a Steam account

If you don't have an account yet, go to [store.steampowered.com](https://store.steampowered.com/) and click **Install Steam** or **Login** → **Create account**.

### Step 2: Join the White Ravens group

1. Go to the [White Ravens Gaming](https://steamcommunity.com/groups/whiteravensgaming) page.
2. Click **Join Group**.
3. Done — you're a member!

### Step 3: Enjoy the community

- Browse discussions and events on the group page.
- Check who from the group is currently online and play together.
- Join organized gaming sessions.

## Frequently asked questions

??? question "Is Steam free?"
    Yes, a Steam account is free. You only pay for games you want to buy (but there are also many free-to-play games).

??? question "Do I need to install the Steam app?"
    For gaming — yes, you need the Steam client on your computer. But for browsing the group and discussions, a browser is enough.

??? question "How do I find other White Ravens members in a game?"
    After joining the Steam group, you'll see the member list. You can also check who is online and send a game invite.
