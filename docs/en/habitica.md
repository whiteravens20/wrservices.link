# White Ravens Habitica

White Ravens Habitica is a free app that **turns your daily tasks and habits into a game**. Based on the [Habitica](https://habitica.com/) platform, but privately hosted — no ads and no paid features thanks to the [awinterstein/habitica](https://github.com/awinterstein/habitica) repository. All features are available for free.

!!! tip "Link"
    [White Ravens Habitica](https://habitica.wrservices.link/)

**Key features:**

- :material-sword: **Gamification** — complete tasks, earn experience points, and level up
- :material-checkbox-marked: **Task list** — organize daily duties, habits, and goals
- :material-trophy: **Rewards** — unlock achievements and virtual rewards for consistency
- :material-account-group: **Challenges** — compete and collaborate with other users
- :material-cash-off: **Free** — all features available at no cost

---

## How does it work?

Habitica treats your daily duties like quests in an RPG game:

- **Completed a task?** → You get experience points and gold
- **Skipped a habit?** → Your character loses health
- **Level up** → Unlock new elements (e.g., armor, pets)

## How to get started?

### Step 1: Create an account

1. Go to [White Ravens Habitica](https://habitica.wrservices.link/).
2. Register using your email address.
3. Create your character.

### Step 2: Add your tasks

Habitica offers three types of tasks:

| Type | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Habits** | Things you want to do more (or less) often | "Exercise", "Don't drink soda" |
| **Dailies** | Tasks to complete every day (or on specific days) | "Clean desk", "Read 20 pages" |
| **To-dos** | One-time tasks with an optional deadline | "Submit report", "Fix bike" |

### Step 3: Complete tasks and level up

- Check off completed tasks — your character gains experience and gold.
- Spend gold on rewards (e.g., character equipment or real-life rewards you set yourself).
- Level up and discover new game elements.

### Step 4: Join challenges

- Browse community challenges and join ones that motivate you.
- Collaborate with others and compete for rewards.

## Frequently asked questions

??? question "Do I have to pay for any features?"
    No. Our instance has all features unlocked for free — there are no microtransactions or subscriptions.

??? question "Can I use it on my phone?"
    Yes — on Android through the modified app (see the section above). On iOS, you can use it through the mobile browser.

??? question "How is this different from official Habitica?"
    Our instance is functionally identical, but payment options have been removed — everything is free. Data is stored on a private White Ravens server.

??? question "Is my data private?"
    Yes. Your tasks and progress are stored on the White Ravens server, not with any external company.
