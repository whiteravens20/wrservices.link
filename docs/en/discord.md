# Discord

Discord is a free platform for text, voice, and video conversations. The **White Ravens** Discord server is the main meeting place for our community — here we play, discuss, and develop projects together.

!!! tip "Join us"
    [White Ravens Discord invite link](https://discord.gg/5JMk8Z4)

**What you'll find on our server:**

- :material-message-text: **Topic channels** — separate channels for games, technology, announcements, and casual conversations
- :material-microphone: **Voice chats** — join a voice channel and talk live
- :material-bell: **Announcements** — stay up to date with White Ravens news
- :material-account-group: **Community** — meet people with similar interests

---

## How to join?

### Step 1: Create a Discord account

If you don't have an account yet, go to [discord.com](https://discord.com/) and sign up — you just need an email address.

### Step 2: Join the White Ravens server

Click the [invite link](https://discord.gg/5JMk8Z4) — you'll automatically join our server.

### Step 3: Look around

After joining, you'll see the channel list on the left side:

- **Text channels** (# icon) — for writing messages
- **Voice channels** (:material-volume-high: icon) — for live conversations
- **Announcements** — important information from administration

### Step 4: Start chatting

Type a message in your chosen text channel or join a voice channel by clicking on its name.

## Useful tips

!!! info "Official guide"
    For new users: [Getting Started with Discord](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360045138571-Beginner-s-Guide-to-Discord) — official step-by-step guide.

- **Notifications:** You can mute channels that don't interest you (right-click → Mute channel).
- **Mobile app:** Discord is available on [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.discord) and [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/discord/id985746746) — conversations sync across devices.
- **Problems?** Write on the help channel or contact the administration.

## Frequently asked questions

??? question "Is Discord free?"
    Yes. All basic features (chats, voice calls, video) are free.

??? question "Do I need to install the app?"
    No — Discord also works in the browser at [discord.com/app](https://discord.com/app). But the desktop and mobile apps perform better.

??? question "How do I change my nickname on the server?"
    Right-click on your name in the member list → **Edit Server Profile** → change your nickname.
